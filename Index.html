About Our Kalari (English)

VS Kalari Marma Chikilsa is a traditional martial arts training center dedicated to teaching Kalaripayattu and Marma Chikilsa to children and students. Training is only for students (not for adults).

Location: MVF8+57, Keezhthonnakkal, Kerala 695313
